**POV:** Diane Foster, first person
**Setting:** Bayshore Boardwalk after Saturday afternoon performance
**Time:** Late afternoon

**Purpose:** Create the complication where Diane gets what she thought she wanted and must now face the consequences.

**Key Details:** Saturday afternoon as peak beach time, Peter's casual confidence in asking her out, the status symbol of Peter's attention from other girls' perspectives, period dating rituals and preparation, parent meeting date at door, the Chevy as symbol of teen independence and romance.
